`conf`
----------

**Type:** `array`

**Value:** `["<type-str>", "<name-str>"]`

**Attribute:** Optional

**Parent:** [main_conf](main_conf.md)

Global configuration item for nginx module. The supported types of `<type-str>`:  

- `int`  
- `size`  
- `time`  
- `bool`  
- `string`
