`handler`
----------

**Type:** `object`

**Value:** `{ "<key-str>": <val>, "<key-str>": <val>, ... , "<key-str>": <val> }`

**Attribute:** Required

**Parent:** [handlers](handlers.md)

Handler for `uri` in nginx module. You can set `<key-str>` as below:  

- [`uri`](uri.md)

- [`action_for_request_body`](action_for_request_body.md)

- [`methods`](methods.md)

- [`upstream`](upstream.md)
