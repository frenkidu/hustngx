get
----------

**Interface:** `/get`

**Method:** `GET`

**Argument:** 

*  **key**  (Required)   
*  **type**  (Required)   
The value refers to [`value-types`](types.md)  

**Sample:**

    curl -i -X GET "http://localhost:8085/get?key=test_key&type=string"
